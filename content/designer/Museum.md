The Museum is a people watching look watch at the setting of an art museum. In
this museum the exhibits are not the true art. The guest are. In a living museum inspired
take the guests are alive when the light shines down from the artistic heavens upon them
where as the part of the stage where the action is not taking placed is flooded in a land of
darkness reflecting an idea that a museum is only truly alive through the guests that are
within it when it is night time and no one beyond the guards are in a museum the museum
is not truly alive. The exhibits themselves are lit up using floor light as to make themselves
separate from the guests who are truly the centerpiece of the show. The end of the
exhibition marks the museum returning to the normalcy realistic museum the museum is
normally is. Purged of the interesting guests and magnificent art whose dance of a life is a
wonder to behold.
